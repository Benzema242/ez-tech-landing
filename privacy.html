Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

EZ Tech Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") operates the EZ Tech Solutions website and appointment booking application. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Submit a booking — first and last name, phone number, email address, requested service(s), preferred date and time, and any notes you include.
  • Send a contact message — name, email address, phone number, and your message.

We do not collect payment information, and we do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and manage our services:

  • To confirm, schedule, and manage your service appointment.
  • To contact you by phone or email regarding your booking or enquiry.
  • To send you a booking confirmation email when you provide an email address.
  • To send our team an internal notification of your booking request.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Booking records are stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database service. Data is stored on servers hosted by Amazon Web Services (AWS). Supabase enforces row-level security, meaning your data is only accessible through authenticated, authorised requests.

Email notifications are sent via Resend, a transactional email service. Resend processes your name and email address to deliver confirmation emails. Resend's privacy policy is available at resend.com.

4. Data Retention

We retain booking records for as long as necessary to provide our services and for reasonable business record-keeping purposes. If you would like your records removed, contact us at the address below and we will delete your information promptly.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@ez-techgroup.com.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external services (WhatsApp, Google Reviews). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
